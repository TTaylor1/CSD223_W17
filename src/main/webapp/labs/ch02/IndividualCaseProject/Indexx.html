World of Warcraft:

World of Warcraft is an MMORPG(Massive Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game), this game has many features to it to keep people entertained while playing. First it requires you to choose between 2 different factions. You can be either Horde(Animalistic, Bloody) or Alliance(Religious, do-gooders)Each faction has their own races you can choose from. Once you choose the faction and race you want, it now gives you the option of choosing which class you want to play, their are 12 classes to choose from now, and races can only play certain classes, so choose wisely...


Once that is all chosen, you can now continue into the land of Azeroth, where Weeks upon Months of non stop grinding and farming awaits for you to endure.

CounterStrike: Global Offensive

CounterStrike is a FPS(First Person Shooter), Their are a few different game types you can play. These include Gun Game where you need to get a kill with each type of weapon until someone gets a kill with every type and wins the match. Free For All: where you are on your own, and have to shoot everyone else, the person with the most points at the end of the game wins. Casual/Competitive are roughly the exact same, theirs attackers and defenders on either side of the map. Casual has up to 15 rounds, with the winner being determined at 8. Competitive however, you can get better ranks, which this can go to 30 rounds, where the winner is the first team to 16 wins, or it is a Tie at 15 wins each.

Ark: Survival Evolved

Ark:Survival Evolved is a Sandbox type game, you awake on an unknown island, surrounded by dinosaurs, and large creatures. You are determined to survive in this new world, so you start out by crafting supplies such as a hatchet, a pickaxe, a campfire. You then create a bow and some arrows, to then create some tranqulizers to be able to knock out and tame a dinosaur for protection. Eventually it goes from surviving, to dominating the most powerful dino's on the island. Then all of a sudden you run into another player, or Tribe of players, who might be aggressive, so you will have to find a way to defend yourself, and your supplies from the attackers, in the end no matter how powerful you get in the game, it all ends up being kill or be killed...
